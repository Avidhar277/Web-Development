Narula Institute of Technology


The institute is affiliated to MAKAUT, WB and the technical courses are approved by AICTE. The Institute has received prestigious NIRF ranking given by MHRD, Govt of India for the last 4 consecutive years. This year the Institute has ranked in the rank band of 201-250 in the Engineering category. It has NBA accredited degree programmes in Engineering. It also prides itself with the prestigious NAAC accreditation. The institute was deemed worthy of receiving the notable World Bank assisted and MHRD approved TEQIP (phase 2) grant for advancement of Technical education. It has also been deemed eligible for receiving central assistance under recognition of clauses 2(f) and 12(b) under UGC Act.

B.Tech(Electrical)

Electronics engineering is a broader field that deals with analog electronics, consumer electronics, embedded systems, digital electronics and power electronics.

EE students may learn about electrical machines, power systems, controls and instrumentation, power electronics and power systems operation and control.

B.Tech(Mechanical)

Mechanical engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the principles of physics and materials science for analysis, design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

It is the branch of engineering that involves the production and usage of heat and mechanical power for the design, production, and operation of machines and tools.
